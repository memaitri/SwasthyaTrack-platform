# Health Card Edit Approval - User Guide

## For Class Teachers

### What Changed?
Every time you edit a health card, your changes now require Headmaster approval - even if the card was already approved before.

### Why This Change?
This ensures all health data is reviewed and verified by the Headmaster, maintaining data quality and accuracy for student health records.

### How It Works

#### Editing a Health Card:

1. **Navigate to Health Cards**
   - Go to Health Cards page
   - Find the student's health card you want to edit

2. **Make Your Changes**
   - Click the "Edit" button
   - Update any fields that need correction or updating
   - Fill in all required information

3. **Submit for Approval**
   - Click "Save"
   - You'll see: **"Health card updated - Pending approval"**
   - Message: "Your changes have been submitted and are pending Headmaster approval."

4. **Wait for Approval**
   - The card status will show as "Pending"
   - Headmaster will review your changes
   - You'll be notified once approved or rejected

#### If Your Edit is Rejected:

1. **Check Rejection Reason**
   - View the health card to see why it was rejected
   - Read the Headmaster's feedback

2. **Make Corrections**
   - Edit the card again
   - Address the issues mentioned in rejection reason
   - Resubmit for approval

3. **Resubmit**
   - Save your corrections
   - Card goes back to pending approval

### Important Notes:

- ✅ You can edit a health card multiple times before it's approved
- ✅ Each edit resets the approval status to "Pending"
- ✅ Only the latest version is reviewed by the Headmaster
- ✅ You can view pending cards but cannot edit them until approved/rejected
- ⚠️ Make sure all information is accurate before submitting
- ⚠️ Double-check your entries to avoid multiple rejection cycles

### Best Practices:

1. **Review Before Saving**: Check all fields before submitting
2. **Complete Information**: Fill in all available health data
3. **Accurate Measurements**: Ensure weight, height, BP are correct
4. **Clear Notes**: Add relevant notes for the Headmaster
5. **Timely Updates**: Submit health cards promptly after health checkups

## For Headmasters

### What Changed?
You now receive approval requests not just for new health cards, but also for any edits made to existing cards by Class Teachers.

### Why This Change?
This gives you complete oversight of all health data changes, ensuring data integrity and quality control.

### How It Works

#### Reviewing Updated Health Cards:

1. **Access Approvals Queue**
   - Navigate to Approvals page
   - You'll see both new and updated health cards marked as "Pending"

2. **Review Changes**
   - Click on a pending health card
   - Review all health information
   - Check for accuracy and completeness

3. **Approve or Reject**
   
   **To Approve:**
   - Click "Approve" button
   - Card status changes to "Approved"
   - Class Teacher is notified
   
   **To Reject:**
   - Click "Reject" button
   - Enter a clear rejection reason
   - Explain what needs to be corrected
   - Class Teacher receives your feedback

#### Managing Your Approval Queue:

1. **Filter by Status**
   - View only pending cards
   - See approved/rejected history
   - Filter by year or class

2. **Prioritize Reviews**
   - Review critical health issues first
   - Check cards with referrals
   - Process in order of submission

3. **Bulk Processing**
   - Review multiple cards in one session
   - Approve straightforward cases quickly
   - Take time with complex health issues

### Important Notes:

- ✅ You see all edits made to health cards
- ✅ Each edit requires your approval
- ✅ You can reject with specific feedback
- ✅ Class Teachers can resubmit after corrections
- ⚠️ Review carefully - approved data affects student health tracking
- ⚠️ Provide clear rejection reasons to help Class Teachers

### Best Practices:

1. **Regular Reviews**: Check approval queue daily
2. **Clear Feedback**: Provide specific rejection reasons
3. **Verify Data**: Cross-check measurements and health indicators
4. **Check Referrals**: Ensure referral recommendations are appropriate
5. **Document Issues**: Note any patterns of errors for training

### What to Look For:

- ✅ Accurate measurements (weight, height, BMI)
- ✅ Correct blood pressure readings
- ✅ Appropriate referral recommendations
- ✅ Complete health history
- ✅ Proper documentation of conditions
- ❌ Unrealistic measurements
- ❌ Missing required fields
- ❌ Incorrect calculations
- ❌ Inappropriate referrals

   - Changes save immediately
   - No approval required

2. **Override Capability**
   - Fix data issues quickly

   - Manage exceptional cases

### Important Notes:

- ✅ Admin edits bypass approval workflow
- ✅ Use this power responsibly
- ✅ Document significant changes
- ⚠️ Only use for system management and corrections
- ⚠️ Don't bypass approval for routine updates

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What happens to already approved health cards?
**A:** They remain approved until a Class Teacher edits them. Any edit resets the status to "Pending" and requires HM approval again.

### Q: Can I edit a card multiple times before approval?
**A:** Yes, Class Teachers can edit pending cards multiple times. Only the latest version is reviewed by the Headmaster.

### Q: How long does approval take?
**A:** This depends on your Headmaster's review schedule. Contact your HM if urgent approval is needed.

### Q: What if I made a mistake after submitting?
**A:** You can edit the card again before the HM approves it. The latest version will be reviewed.

### Q: Can I see why my card was rejected?
**A:** Yes, the rejection reason is displayed on the health card. Read it carefully and make corrections.

### Q: Do Admins need approval for their edits?
**A:** No, Admin edits are applied immediately without requiring approval.

### Q: Will I be notified when my card is approved?
**A:** The system updates the status immediately. Check the Health Cards page to see approval status.

### Q: Can I view pending cards?
**A:** Yes, you can view pending cards but cannot edit them until they're approved or rejected.
